# **Appeals Review Board Guide Book**

> **Appeals Review Board - A.R.B.**

> **This document is for internal A.R.B. operations only.**

> **Do not share this guidebook with anyone outside A.R.B.**

> **Sharing this document with anyone outside A.R.B. may result <ins>consequences</ins> for you**

### **What A.R.B. is**

- **A.R.B. is <ins>the</ins> committee of "PANTSIR", our main goal is to be <ins>helpful</ins> to community members of "PANTSIR".**

- **A.R.B. <ins>reviews</ins> all types of <ins>appeals</ins> related to "PANTSIR".**

- **We are <ins>independent and impartial</ins>, our decisions should not be affected by all types of emotions.**

> **Note: There are other staff branches with their own tasks. We can not <ins>cancel</ins> all of their decisions by our own will.**

> **For example, we can not affect decisions made by "CC Supervisor" regarding "Content Creator" unless the decision involves a "CC" blacklist.**

> **Another example, we can not affect decisions regarding hiring or firing staff members unless the decision involves a staff member blacklist**

### **How you should act as A.R.B.**

1. **Follow "Moderation Handbook"**
2. **Properly write reasons of denials**
3. **Check if a punishment is logged in "mod-actions"**
4. **Ask moderators and players related to an appeal to get more information**
5. **Check a history of punishments related to a player in an appeal**
6. **Check an amount of time which has passed after a punishment**
7. **Observe whether a member admits his guilt or not (if he is guilty)**
> Appeals for bans issued under Rule 2.1 are only permitted **after 30 days have elapsed** since the disciplinary action was applied.
